Servo Wiring

Servo motors have got exactly three connections. One at a potential of +5V, one at 0V (GND) and one for setting the servos position. A servo has a range of 180 degree and the Arduino Servo library lets you control the servos by numbers between 0 and 180. If you want to control a motor with the servo layout, 180 is full speed and 0 is off in most cases. Some motor controllers also require the values to be in a range of 20 to 160.
